HackPDX(schools)

Portland's premier civic hackathon, bringing together software engineers and policymakers to change education.

Who & What Professional developers gather to eat, chat, build projects, and do good. Our theme focuses on schools, primarily at a local level––help us prove that twelve hours can have an impact!

Why HackPDX was dreamed up and put together by two high school students. Our theme is especially dear to us; we’re both passionately involved in tech and software development, but we’re tied to a system that sometimes lags behind that innovation. It was only natural to want to bring together the talent we see at meetups and internships to change that. There’s no question that “STEM” is a buzzword in public education now. Technology in education is okay, but technology for education is still lacking. Many schools are still organized on outdated software, counterintuitive at best and byzantine at worst. We decided that when tech solutions get in the way of learning there’s a broken system to fix.
We’re results-driven. That’s why we’re bringing administrators and educators to the table: the projects you’ll create will have the potential to change lives for the better, and HackPDX is here to make that change happen.{read more}

When & Where Big thanks to New Relic for lending us their amazing space! They’re at 111 SW 5th Ave #2800, nestled on the 29th floor of Portland’s tallest building. The event will run from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM on August 30th, 2014.
