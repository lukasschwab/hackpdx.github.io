HackPDX(schools)

Portland's premier civic hackathon, bringing software engineers and policymakers to change education.

Who & What HackPDX is a day dedicated to 12 straight hours of hacking. You’re invited to bring project ideas, friends, and a desire to have fun.

Why When dreaming up HackPDX, we wanted to pick a theme that was near and dear to our hearts. As high school students we’re constantly dreaming up solutions that would make school a better place for teachers and students. We’re excited to get like minded individuals like yourself together to brainstorm and hack.

When & Where August 30th, 2014. Thanks to New Relic for hosting us. Get ready to hack on the 29th floor of Portland’s tallest building!
